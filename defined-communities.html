Defined Communities

Our Defined Communities make it possible for you to live with a small group of students who share similar goals, identities, or cultural interests. Defined Communities can encourage personal growth, enhance academic achievement, and offer leadership opportunities. We currently offer ten Defined Communities. Six Defined Communities are offered for our first year-students: Women’s Leadership, Social Justice, The Arts, Thrive, Blueprint, and Honors. We offer four defined communities for our upper-class students: Upperclass Honors, Emerging Leaders, Global Living, and Service Learning.

See below for a brief glimpse of each community!
Blueprint Community

The Blueprint Community is housed in Slade Hall and is overseen by 1 RA.

Thrive Community

The Thrive Community is housed in Slade Hall and is overseen by 1 RA.

Womens Leadership Community

The Women's Leadership Community is housed in the Trees Complex and is overseen by 1 RA.

Service Learning Community

The Service Learning Community is housed in Kresge Hall and is overseen by 1 RA.

Emerging Leaders Community

The Emerging Leaders Community is housed in Fenway and is overseen by 1 RA.

Global Living Community

The Global Learning Community is housed in Orchard North and is overseen by 2 RAs.

Social Justice Community

The Social Justice Community is housed in Miller Hall and overseen by 1 RA.

The Arts Community

The Arts Community is housed in Miller Hall and overseen by 1 RA.

The Honors Community

The Honors Community is housed in Slade Hall and overseen by 2 RAs.
